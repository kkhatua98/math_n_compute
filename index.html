Hi, I'm Kaustav

Machine Learning Engineer

I host the Getting Simple podcast on lifestyle design, creativity, tech & culture, record educational videos on Creative AI & Coding, and publish Sketches & Mini-Essays. I studied design technology at the Harvard Graduate School of Design and worked exploring the role of AI in architecture, engineering, and construction at Autodesk. My work focuses on developing intuitive tools for creatives and how the collaboration between human and artificial intelligences can enhance the design process. Previously, I studied architecture and worked in the design and delivery of complex architectural geometries at award-winning firms such as AR-MA and Foster + Partners.
